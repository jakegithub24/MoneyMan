The 50/30/20 Rule

A simple budgeting guideline: Allocate 50% of monthly income to Needs (bills, food), 30% to Wants (entertainment), and 20% to Savings or paying off debt.

Emergency Funds

Set aside 3 to 6 months of living expenses into an easily accessible safety-net fund. This protects you from having to borrow high-interest loans in case of emergencies.

Read details

Debt Snowball

A debt reduction strategy where you pay off your debts in order of smallest to largest balance. This builds positive behavioral momentum with early, quick victories!
