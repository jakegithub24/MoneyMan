# 1. PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## 1.1 Problem Statement
Students, financially struggling individuals, and elderly users often lack a simple, non‑intimidating tool to track spending, save money, and understand their finances. Existing apps are either too complex, too feature‑heavy, or not designed for emotional support and education.

## 1.2 Vision
MoneyMan is a friendly, AI‑assisted money manager that focuses on **financial well‑being** rather than just numbers. It gently guides users towards better habits through simple logging, actionable insights, and education.

## 1.3 Target Personas
- **Riya (19, student)** – needs easy expense tracking and peer‑like budget templates.
- **Sunil (34, gig worker)** – irregular income; needs emergency savings nudges and bill reminders.
- **Smt. Sharma (68, retired)** – wants safety, large text, and family oversight.

## 1.4 Core Value Proposition
- **Quick logging** with AI category suggestions.
- **Visual budgets** that show progress instantly.
- **EMI & bill reminders** to avoid penalties.
- **Monthly GenAI analysis** that explains spending in plain language.
- **Financial literacy** in bite‑sized tips.

## 1.5 MVP Feature Scope (must‑have)
Refer to `features.md` for the full MVP checklist. Top‑level:
1. Transaction logging with smart categorization
2. Monthly category budgets with alerts
3. Savings goals tracker
4. EMI tracker & calculator
5. Dashboard with key metrics
6. AI‑generated monthly insights
7. Bill/EMI push notifications
8. Offline‑first, local data encryption
9. Light/dark mode, accessible UI
10. Basic glossary & tips

## 1.6 Success Metrics (MVP Launch)
- 60% of users log at least one transaction per week after onboarding.
- Budget adherence improves by 10% within 2 months (self‑reported).
- EMI reminder feature reduces late payments by 30%.
- App store rating ≥ 4.0 within first 100 reviews.
